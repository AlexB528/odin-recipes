Pasta with butter and onion

Description

This recipe is very simple. Melt a stick of butter in blended tomatoes, add salt, and then cut an onion in half and put it in the sauce. Let this simmer for 45 minutes before mixing into pasta.

Inredients

  • 2 cups canned tomatoes
  • 5 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 - 1.5 pounds of pasta
  • salt

Steps

  1. blend tomates
  2. melt 5 tablespoons of butter in tomatoes
  3. mix in salt
  4. out onion in
  5. simmer for 45m
  6. mix into cooked pasta
