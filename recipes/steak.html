Steak

Description

Steak with butter and rosemary.

Inredients

  • Steak
  • Butter
  • vegetable oil
  • 2 Rosemary Sprigs
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Get pan very hot
  2. pour oil in pan
  3. place steak in pan (making it fall away from you
  4. in the last 3 minutes, put rosemary sprig over steak along with butter
  5. baste steak for remaining 3 minutes, pour butter over the rosemary sprig
  6. let steak rest for 5-10 minutes
