Chilli

Description

Beef & black bean chilli!

Inredients

  • 2 cups canned tomatoes
  • 500g black beans
  • ground beef
  • 1 medium onion
  • paprika
  • chilli powder
  • oregano
  • bay leaf
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. resoak the beans over night
  2. brown the beef
  3. add in onion, tomatoe, and seasonings
  4. bring to a boil and then simmer for 45m
  5. add beans in last 15 minutes
